Tori Johnson

Hello my name is Tori Johnson. I have been coaching for 27 years. I started my coaching career in Southern California at Duarte High School. We moved to Central California where I have coached at Golden West, Woodlake and Mt Whitney. Some high lights would be at Golden West the team of 1997 won the schools 1st league title. At Woodlake High School the team of 2013 won Valley. The following year we returned to Valley and fell short but it resulted in a move up in divisions. At Mt whitney we were Co League Champs in 2016. The titles are great but my greatest reward is working with young ladies. It brings me great joy to help the athletes reach their personal best. In addition I take great pride in teaching girls the pillars of character and Victory with Honor.

Da Kine Ohana is a club with a family culture. We have players from twelve years old to eighteen years old. We have athletes that come from different schools. Though we are from different schools we come together as one OHANA! This year the DKO coaching staff consisted of nine coaches. Not counting myself five of our eight coaches are previous players of mine. Blessed to be OHANA!
